How Capitec Pay works at betting sites

Capitec Pay is a real-time payment solution built into the Capitec banking app. When you choose Capitec Pay at a betting site's deposit page, the transaction is authenticated directly through your app. You receive a push notification, approve it with your PIN or biometrics, and the funds arrive in your betting wallet instantly.

The process does not require you to enter card details or share your online banking login credentials with the bookmaker. Authentication stays within the Capitec app environment, which makes it one of the more secure local payment options.

Step-by-step: how to deposit

  1. Log in to your betting site account and go to the cashier or deposit page.
  2. Select Capitec Pay from the list of payment options.
  3. Enter the amount you want to deposit (check the minimum).
  4. The page will redirect or generate a reference. Open your Capitec banking app.
  5. Approve the pending payment in the Capitec Pay section.
  6. Funds should appear in your betting wallet within seconds.

Why Capitec Pay is popular with South African bettors

Capitec is South Africa's largest retail bank by customer count. Because so many bettors already use Capitec for everyday banking, the payment method requires no additional sign-up or app installation. It is instant, free at most bookmakers, and does not involve entering card details on a third-party site.
