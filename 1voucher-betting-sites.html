Why 1Voucher suits some bettors

1Voucher is a prepaid cash-to-digital method. You buy a physical voucher at a retail store and use the code to make an online deposit. Because you pay cash for the voucher before using it, you cannot accidentally overspend. This makes 1Voucher a useful tool for bettors who prefer tight spending control or who do not have convenient access to internet banking or a debit card.

Spending control

Unlike card or EFT payments, a 1Voucher deposit is limited to the value of the voucher you purchased. There is no way to spend more than the physical voucher is worth. This built-in cap makes it a practical tool for budget-conscious bettors.

No bank account required for deposits

You can deposit using 1Voucher without a bank account, as long as you can buy the voucher with cash at a retail outlet. For users who are not fully banked, this can be the most accessible deposit route.

Withdrawals go via bank transfer

Winnings cannot be paid back to a 1Voucher. All withdrawals at South African bookmakers go to a verified bank account. If you plan to withdraw winnings, you will need to provide bank account details during the verification process.
