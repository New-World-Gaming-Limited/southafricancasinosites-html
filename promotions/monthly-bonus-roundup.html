How to evaluate a bonus

The real value of a bonus is not the headline amount - it is the expected cash you can withdraw after completing the wagering requirement. A useful approximation:

Expected value ≈ (Bonus amount ÷ Wagering requirement) × Win rate

At typical sports betting win rates (55%), a R1,000 bonus with 3x wagering yields approximately R183 in expected withdrawable cash. This assumes you bet at minimum qualifying odds without accumulator strategy. A 3x requirement is significantly better than 6x or 8x which the same formula yields approximately R61-R92 respectively.
