Live betting on Football

Live (in-play) football betting is available at all 14 bookmakers reviewed. Betway offers the most markets during live play, including the ability to cash out accumulator bets mid-game. Odds update in near-real time. Live streaming of selected PSL matches is available at Betway. Other bookmakers do not offer live streaming but do provide live score widgets inside their apps.

Season and event calendar

The Betway Premiership runs from August to May. The PSL Cup competition and Carling Knockout add additional matches from September. The UEFA Champions League group stage begins in September, with knockout rounds from February. The Africa Cup of Nations takes place every two years. The 2026 FIFA World Cup in Canada, Mexico and the United States will generate the highest betting volumes South African bookmakers have ever seen.
