How to claim your welcome bonus

  1. Choose an operator from the comparison table above and click the visit link.
  2. Complete registration with your full name, ID number, date of birth and contact details.
  3. Enter any promo code if one is required. Check the promo code field during registration or on the deposit page.
  4. Verify your account by submitting a copy of your ID and proof of address if prompted.
  5. Make your qualifying deposit at or above the minimum threshold stated in the bonus terms.
  6. Check your wallet for the bonus credit, which should appear within a few minutes to an hour.

Before placing any bet with bonus funds, re-read the minimum odds requirement and wagering multiplier. Placing bets below the qualifying odds will not count toward clearing the bonus.
