Season and event calendar

Super Rugby Pacific runs from February to June. The United Rugby Championship runs August to June. The Rugby Championship (Springboks vs All Blacks, Wallabies, Pumas) runs July to October. The British & Irish Lions tour South Africa periodically. The Rugby World Cup takes place every four years (2027 in Australia). Currie Cup matches provide year-round domestic coverage.
