How to get your withdrawal faster

Complete KYC before you request your first withdrawal

Identity verification is the single biggest cause of withdrawal delays. Most South African bookmakers require a copy of your national ID or passport and proof of address. Submit these documents as soon as you register rather than waiting until you want to withdraw. Betway and Hollywoodbets both allow early document submission through their account dashboards.

Use the same bank account you deposited from

Most bookmakers in South Africa withdraw only to bank accounts that match the account used for deposits. If you deposit via Capitec Pay or Ozow, the withdrawal will usually go to the same underlying bank account. Using a different bank account can trigger a manual review that adds several business days.

Request withdrawals on business days

Bank processing in South Africa does not run at weekends or on public holidays. A withdrawal requested on a Friday afternoon may not reach your bank until Tuesday. Planning requests for Monday to Thursday mornings gives you the best chance of same-week receipt.

Keep withdrawal amounts within standard limits

Very large withdrawals, particularly from new accounts or following unusual betting activity, are more likely to be reviewed manually. If you are withdrawing a larger amount, expect to provide additional documentation and allow extra time.
