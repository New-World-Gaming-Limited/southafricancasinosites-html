Powers and Functions

Regulates gambling, liquor, and tourism in Mpumalanga. The MER is one of the most active licensing authorities for online bookmakers alongside the WCGRB. Issues licences and conducts compliance enforcement.

How to Check a Bookmaker's Licence

Contact the MER at 013 752 8099 or info@mer.org.za. The MER maintains records of all licensed operators in Mpumalanga.

How to File a Complaint

Email info@mer.org.za or call 013 752 8099. The MER investigates complaints against licensed operators and can impose administrative penalties.

Recent Developments

Mpumalanga continues to be a major online bookmaker licensing hub alongside the Western Cape. Multiple national operators hold MER licences, including Hollywoodbets, Supabets, and Betfred.
