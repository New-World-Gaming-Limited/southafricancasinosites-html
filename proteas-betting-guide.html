Proteas cricket formats and markets

Test Cricket

South Africa hosts and tours Test series against England, India, Australia, New Zealand, Bangladesh, Sri Lanka and Pakistan. Markets: match winner (including draw), series winner, innings runs, top batsman, player of the match.

SA20 (T20 Franchise)

The SA20 league runs January-February and features six city-based franchises with international players. Strong domestic betting interest. Markets: match winner, top team batsman, over/under runs, outright winner.

ICC Events

T20 World Cup (biennial), ODI World Cup, Champions Trophy and World Test Championship all feature the Proteas. Outright markets open months in advance at Betway and 10bet.
