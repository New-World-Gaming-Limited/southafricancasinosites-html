How to compare betting bonuses

A bigger bonus number is not always better. Evaluate each offer using these five metrics:

  1. Turnover (wagering) requirement: How many times must you bet the bonus before withdrawal? 3x is reasonable. 8x or above makes the bonus difficult to clear.
  2. Minimum odds: Must bets qualify at certain odds? 1.50 is achievable. 3.0 or above is restrictive for most bettors.
  3. Deposit threshold: How much must you deposit to qualify? A R1,000 bonus requiring a R500 deposit suits most. A R5,000 bonus requiring a R2,500 deposit suits fewer.
  4. Expiry period: How long do you have to use the bonus? 7 days is short. 30 days is standard.
  5. Withdrawal cap: Is there a maximum amount you can withdraw from bonus winnings? A low cap reduces the effective value.
