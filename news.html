Regulatory bodies we track

National Gambling Board (NGB)

National oversight and coordination of provincial gambling regulations under the National Gambling Act 2004.

Gauteng Gambling Board (GGB)

Licences and regulates casinos, bookmakers, bingo and LPMs in Gauteng province.

Western Cape Gambling and Racing Board (WCGRB)

Regulates all gambling including online sports betting operators based in Western Cape.

KwaZulu-Natal Gaming and Betting Board (KZNGBB)

Regulates casinos, betting and gaming in KwaZulu-Natal.

National Lotteries Commission (NLC)

Regulates the National Lottery operated by Ithuba Holdings and administers good-cause distributions.

Financial Intelligence Centre (FIC)

Oversees anti-money laundering compliance for gambling operators, including KYC and reporting obligations.
