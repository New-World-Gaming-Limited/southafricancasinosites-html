About Montecasino

Montecasino is a Tsogo Sun Entertainment property, part of South Africa's largest casino and hotel group. The 56,000 m² complex opened in 2000 and was designed by international architects to replicate an Italian Renaissance village complete with cobbled streets, painted sky ceilings, piazzas and campaniles. It attracts an estimated 5 million visitors annually.

The casino sits within a larger entertainment precinct that is open to all ages. The gaming floor is segregated and access is restricted to those 18 and older. The rest of the complex - restaurants, cinema, theatre, bowling - is accessible to all visitors.

The casino

Montecasino's gaming floor is one of the largest in Gauteng. The floor features:

  • 1,800+ slot machines: Standard video slots, penny slots, high-denomination machines, progressive jackpots and multi-line video poker.
  • 60+ table games: Blackjack (multiple variants), American and European roulette, baccarat, three-card poker, Casino Hold 'Em and various side bets.
  • Poker room: Dedicated poker room with cash games and regular tournament schedule.
  • Slots Club: Player rewards programme offering points, cashback and exclusive events.

The casino is open 24 hours. Valid ID is required for entry. Dress code is smart casual.

The Palazzo Hotel

The Palazzo Hotel is a 5-star property with 246 rooms and suites. Room types range from Classic Rooms to Executive Suites and the Presidential Suite. Facilities include:

  • Outdoor heated pool and sun deck.
  • Spa with massage, facial and body treatment services.
  • On-site restaurants including Tartine brasserie and poolside dining.
  • Business centre and meeting rooms.
  • Direct access to the casino and Montecasino entertainment precinct.

Entertainment

Teatro

Teatro is Montecasino's main theatre, seating 2,000 guests. It hosts major productions including long-running musicals, comedy shows, concerts and special events. Productions in recent years have included Phantom of the Opera, Les Misérables, Chicago and local SA productions.

Cinema

The Cinema Nouveau and Nu Metro multiplexes within the Montecasino complex show mainstream and art-house films. IMAX screens are available.

Bowling

An 8-lane bowling alley is available for casual play and group bookings.

Dining

Montecasino has over 45 restaurants, cafes and bars. Cuisine ranges from South African steakhouses and Italian trattorias to Japanese, Indian and contemporary fusion. Notable options include Moyo, Wombles Steakhouse, Tasha's and a range of fast-casual options.

Who is Montecasino for?

Montecasino suits a wide range of visitors. Couples visiting for a show and dinner, groups celebrating occasions, business travellers staying at Palazzo, gaming enthusiasts spending a day on the floor, and families taking children to bowling or cinema while adults visit the casino. The design makes it an enjoyable space to spend an afternoon or evening even without gambling.

Responsible gambling

Montecasino operates under Gauteng Gambling Board regulation. Voluntary exclusion, SafePlay trained staff and referral to support services are all in place. Call 0800 006 008 for free gambling support (24/7).
