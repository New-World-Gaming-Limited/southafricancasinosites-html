Mobile site vs app: which should you use?

Use the mobile site if…

  • You prefer not to download additional software
  • Your phone storage is limited
  • You use multiple bookmakers and want a consistent browser workflow
  • The bookmaker does not have an app for your device

Use the app if…

  • You want faster load times on slower networks
  • You want push notifications for odds changes and promotions
  • You want a home-screen icon for quick access
  • You bet frequently and value a dedicated interface

A mobile betting app is a piece of software downloaded to your device from the Play Store, App Store, or directly as an APK. A mobile betting site is a website optimised to display and function correctly in a mobile browser such as Chrome or Safari. Both allow you to bet, deposit and withdraw. Apps tend to offer slightly faster load times and push notifications, while mobile sites require no installation.
