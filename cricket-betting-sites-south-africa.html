Season and event calendar

The SA20 runs in January-February. The IPL runs April-May. ICC Test Championship matches are year-round. The ICC Men's T20 World Cup is biennial. South Africa's bilateral home series typically run October-March. The Momentum One Day Cup and CSA4Day Series provide additional domestic content.
