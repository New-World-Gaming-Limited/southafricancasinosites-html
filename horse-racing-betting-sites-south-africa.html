Live betting on Horse Racing

Hollywoodbets offers live streaming of most South African race meetings, making it the clear leader for horse racing. Viewers can watch races and bet in real time from the same interface. Easybet and Sportingbet provide live race results and odds but do not stream video. All bookmakers settle horse racing bets immediately after official result confirmation.

Season and event calendar

South African horse racing runs year-round. The most prestigious meetings are the J&B Met (January, Kenilworth), the Durban July (July, Greyville) and the Summer Cup (November, Turffontein). International racing including the Cheltenham Festival (March) and Royal Ascot (June) is covered at most bookmakers. The Grand National (UK) attracts the single highest horse racing betting volume of the year.
