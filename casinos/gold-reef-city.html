About Gold Reef City

Gold Reef City was developed by Tsogo Sun on the site of the Crown Mines, one of the deepest gold mines in Johannesburg's history. The resort opened in 1987 and has been expanded multiple times since. The heritage gold mining theme is visible throughout the property in the Victorian-era architecture, mine headgear structures and the Gold Reef City Theme Park.

Unlike most casino resorts, Gold Reef City's theme park is a significant destination in its own right, making it popular for school groups, families and corporate events as well as casino visitors. Entrance to the theme park and entertainment precinct is separate from casino access.

  • 50+ table games including blackjack, roulette (American and European), baccarat, three-card poker and casino poker.
  • VIP gaming room for high-stake players.

Gold Reef City Theme Park

The theme park adjacent to the casino features rides for multiple age groups:

  • The Anaconda: A corkscrew roller coaster, one of the park's signature thrill rides.
  • Drop Zone: A vertical drop tower from approximately 30 metres.
  • Mine Train: A family-friendly coaster themed around mine cart transport.
  • Victorian Ferris Wheel: A large observation wheel with views across southern Johannesburg.
  • Children's rides: Multiple age-appropriate attractions for younger visitors.

Theme park tickets are purchased separately from casino access. Family day packages are available.

Gold Reef City Hotel

The 4-star Gold Reef City Hotel is themed in the Victorian mining era aesthetic. It offers standard rooms and suites with direct access to the entertainment precinct. Facilities include a swimming pool, restaurant and bar, fitness centre and conference rooms. The hotel is a popular choice for family stays and corporate bookings near the Nasrec Expo Centre.
