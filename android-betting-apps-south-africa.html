Play Store apps vs APK downloads

In South Africa, some licensed bookmakers list native apps on the Google Play Store while others distribute Android APK files directly from their websites. Both are legitimate when the bookmaker holds a provincial gambling licence.

Google Play Store Apps

Apps from Betway, Hollywoodbets and Gbets can be found on the Play Store. Advantage: automatic updates and malware scanning. Check the developer name matches the bookmaker's official brand before installing.

Direct APK Downloads

Easybet, Supabets, Lulabet and TicTacBets distribute APK files from their official websites. You need to allow installation from unknown sources on your device. Only download from the bookmaker's own domain.

Mobile-Optimised Sites

YesPlay and ZARbet do not offer a native Android app but their mobile websites are responsive and function well through Chrome on Android. You can add the site to your home screen as a shortcut.

How to install a betting APK on Android

  1. Visit the bookmaker's official website on your Android phone.
  2. Locate the Android app download link (usually under a 'Download App' or 'Mobile' section).
  3. Before tapping download, go to Settings > Security > Install unknown apps and allow your browser.
  4. Download the APK file and open it when prompted.
  5. Follow the on-screen installation steps.
  6. After installing, re-lock the unknown sources permission as a security measure.

What makes a good Android betting app?

Registration speed

A good app completes new account registration in under three minutes without forcing a browser redirect.

Bet slip reliability

Selections should persist across navigation. Odds should refresh in real time. Bet placement should not time out on slower networks.

Deposit and withdrawal

Capitec Pay, Ozow and Instant EFT should work natively inside the app without redirecting to a browser.

Live betting performance

In-play markets should load without lag. Live scores should update without requiring a manual refresh.

Data efficiency

On South African mobile networks, data costs matter. A well-optimised app keeps page weight low without sacrificing functionality.

Responsible gambling tools

Deposit limits, cooling-off periods and self-exclusion must be accessible inside the app, not only on desktop.
