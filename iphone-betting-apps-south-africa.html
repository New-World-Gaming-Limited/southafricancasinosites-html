How to add a betting site shortcut to your iPhone home screen

For bookmakers without an App Store listing, the mobile website can be pinned to your iPhone home screen for a near-native experience:

  1. Open Safari on your iPhone and navigate to the bookmaker's website.
  2. Tap the Share button (the box with an upward arrow) at the bottom of the screen.
  3. Scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen.
  4. Give the shortcut a name and tap Add.
  5. The icon will appear on your home screen and open directly to the site in a full-screen view.
